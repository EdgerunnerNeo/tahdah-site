About the Firm

TAH-DAH Ventures partners with early-stage entrepreneurs to build category-defining companies.

We invest at the intersection of health, sustainability, and function — backing food & beverage brands and disruptive ag-tech companies transforming the global food system.

Our portfolio spans food technology, beverage brands, restaurant technology, and food-delivery platforms across the United States and beyond.
