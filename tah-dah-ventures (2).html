About Early-Stage Venture Capital · Wyoming, USA

TAH-DAH Ventures partners with early-stage entrepreneurs to build category-defining companies.

We invest at the intersection of health, sustainability, and function — backing food & beverage brands and disruptive ag-tech companies transforming the global food system.

Our portfolio spans food technology, beverage brands, restaurant technology, and food-delivery platforms across the United States and beyond.

Team Leadership

Yessi Hernandez

Co-Founder & General Partner

Yessi entered venture capital after a career as a marketing executive, bringing more than a decade of experience in FMCG brand management, marketing, and product development, with leadership roles across the United States and South America. Her work centers on building transformational, sustainable food systems.

She is also the founder of MaterAqua, a non-profit organization working with businesses and communities to move beyond throwaway plastic culture — replacing single-use plastic bottles with reusable alternatives.
